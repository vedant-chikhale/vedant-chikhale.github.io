CN Tower

Elevate every occasion and celebrate infinite possibilities at Canada’s most celebrated architectural icon...the CN Tower.

Discover Toronto’s most spectacular views day or night from floor-to-ceiling panoramic window walls on the Main Observation Level at 346 m/1,136 ft in the sky!

Add to cart
Highlights

  1. Speed up to the 1,136 ft/346m Main Observation Level in a mere 58 seconds in one of six high-speed glass-fronted elevators and take in the stunning views of Toronto.

  2. Elevate your experience using the free Viewfinder app! Use it to identify the city’s most famous landmarks and points of interest as you take in the 360-degree view from our Main Observation Level.

  3. On a clear day, see as far as 160 kilometres (100 miles) all the way to Niagara Falls and into New York State.

Advice for Visitors

Tips

  1. Reservations are recommended for 360 Restaurant; make reservations online or call (416) 362-5411.

  2. Up for an even bigger adventure? Try EdgeWalk, a thrilling outdoor hands-free walk. Cost: Starting at $195 CAD plus tax. More information and reservations: (855) 553-3833 or see details.

  3. CN Tower offers guests free WiFi. Download the free CN Tower Viewfinder app and identify more than 90 landmarks as seen from the Main Observation level. The app offers the choice of spectacular day or night views and comes in 5 languages.

Amenities

  1. Dining: 360 Restaurant, VUE Bistro or Le Café.

  2. Gift Shops: The Gift Shop.
